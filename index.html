He found an old plastic doll floating down the current right where she went under. He tied it to a tree branch. He thought it would keep her quiet. He thought it would stop the noises in his head. It didn't work. One doll wasn't enough to fix the balance.

For fifty straight years he dragged trash out of the waste piles. Ripped plastic limbs. Hollow heads with the eyes missing. Mud stuck inside their synthetic hair. He hung thousands of them from the branches until the whole island was covered in plastic faces staring out at the river. Then they found Julian. In the exact same spot on the shore. Dead in the water. THE LOOP FIXED ITSELF.
